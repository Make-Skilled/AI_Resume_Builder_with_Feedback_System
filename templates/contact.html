Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AI Resume Builder

How does the AI resume generation work?

Our AI analyzes the information you provide and uses advanced language models to create professional, compelling content that highlights your skills and experience effectively.

Is my information secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. Your information is encrypted and we don't store your personal data permanently. Your resume is generated securely and can be downloaded immediately.

What formats can I download my resume in?

You can download your resume in PDF format for printing and professional use, or in text format for online applications and ATS systems.

How accurate is the AI feedback?

Our AI feedback system is trained on thousands of successful resumes and provides detailed, actionable suggestions to improve your resume's effectiveness and ATS compatibility.
